    Venue

    TDM 2023 will take place in the Robarts Library Building at the University of Toronto, St. George Campus. It is a 6-minute walk from St. George subway station. The main entrance is on the ground floor on St. George Street near the intersection of St. George Street and Harbord Street.

    The event will be located on the 4th floor in the Blackburn room.
